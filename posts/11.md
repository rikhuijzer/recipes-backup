# Lasagna

Repeat the following steps until the oven dish is full, with cheese layers in between:

1. About 1 centimeter of [red sauce](#red-sauce).
2. About 0.2 centimeter of **bechamel sauce** (can be Frischecreme natur mix with milk 50/50).
3. One layer of lasagna sheets.

Put **cheese** at two layers in between and one layer on top.

Put in the oven at 200Â°C for about 40 minutes.

## Red sauce

Ensure that veggies are cut small enough.

- **Garlic**
- **Onion**
- **Smoked tofu**
- **Carrot**
- Optional: **Red wine** (at this point cook water out)
- **Tomato sauce** without pieces (because the sauce already has pieces)
- **Tomato purred**
- Optional: **Spinach** instead of tomato and carrot.
- Optional: **Parsley**
- Spices: Italian herbs, salt, pepper, paprika, bit chili, rosmarijn, krauter di province, thyme, oregano
