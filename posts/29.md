# White Cabbage Salad

Based on <https://www.lecker.de/orientalischer-weisskohlsalat-64567.html>:

- Cut the white cabbage in fine slices.
- Massage the cabbage with olive oil, vinegar, and 1 tablespoon sugar, finely grinded ginger.
- Let it stand for 20 minutes.
- Cut fine slices of carrots.
- Optionally cut slices of green salad. (Leave out when you want it to stay good longer.)
- Optionally chickpeas.
- Cranberries cut in half.
- Parsley if available.
- Black sesame seeds pour on top.
